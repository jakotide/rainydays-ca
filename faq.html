FAQ

When can I expect my order?

Delivery time is 4-5 working days in Europe. To the USA, delivery is 7-10 working days. If you are ordering outside of Norway and EU, please check your country's customs charges on imported goods before ordering - we'd hate for you to have any unexpected surprises.

International returns

We currently don't offer free returns outside of Norway, so return postage will need to be covered by yourself. We recommend using a tracked service for your own security, with delivery confirmation.

Which payment methods can I use to pay for my order?

You can use credit/debit card (Visa and Mastercard), Paypal or Klarna. You can also use gift cards!

Can you tell me when a product is back in stock?

We regurarly receive new stock, which will re-appear automatically on the website when it become available. Feel free to sign up to our product notification emails.

Can I collect my order?

We love meeting our customers, but our warehouse is not yet open for the public. We aim to open our first shop soon.

If you still have any questions feel free to contact us here.
